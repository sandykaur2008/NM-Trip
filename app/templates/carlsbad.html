Carlsbad

I got stuck with girl scouts here, but I ended up liking them. They were loud - but also adorable, I couldn't deny it. We walked in a line in the dark, with nothing but lanterns to guide us!

Inside the caverns I found some cool formations that looked like three gurus or video game bosses (you choose).

It was my first time in a cave, and I was thoroughly impressed.
